# Privacy Policy — Life is Easy

**Last updated: March 31, 2026**

## No Data Collection

Life is Easy does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## How it works

- You provide your own OpenAI API key, which is saved **locally on your device** using Chrome's built-in storage.
- When you click a quiz question, the question text is sent directly from your browser to the **OpenAI API** using your own API key.
- No data passes through any third-party server operated by this extension.

## Third-party services

The extension communicates solely with the **OpenAI API** (api.openai.com). Please refer to [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy) for how they handle data.

## Contact

This extension is for personal use. No support contact is provided.
